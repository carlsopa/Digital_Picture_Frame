Welcome to the control panel for the magical picture frame. Here you will be able to have full control and access to your new digital frame.

Use the buttons provided to access the different aspects of the frame. I hope you enjoy your new frame.

You will be able to upload any photos from a computer or mobile device by choosing the "Upload Photos" link below. You can do as many or as few as you would like.

You can manage and delete your photos by choosing "Manage Photos". Each photo will be shown, with a button on the side that allows you the ability to delete the photo.
**WARNING** Once you delete it, it will be gone. If you want it back, you will have to re-upload it.
